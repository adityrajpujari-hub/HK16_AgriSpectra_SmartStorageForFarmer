Government Support & Useful Resources for Farmers

The Government of India provides several schemes and infrastructure support to reduce post-harvest losses and strengthen agricultural storage. Farmers can explore the following official resources.

Storage & Infrastructure Support

  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) — Long-term financing for cold storage, warehouses, silos and post-harvest infrastructure. agriinfra.dac.gov.in — Registration
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) — Grants and support for food processing, cold chains and storage projects. PMKSY on MoFPI — SAMPADA portal
  • Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) — Policy and program details for cold chain and storage schemes. mofpi.gov.in

Digital & Market Support

  • e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) — Online market platform to trade across mandis and reduce storage time. enam.gov.in
  • Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC) — Support for FPOs and farmer groups to access markets and storage solutions. sfacindia.com

Financial & Farmer Support

  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) — Direct income support for farmers. pmkisan.gov.in
  • Crop Insurance & Credit — Many states and insurers offer crop insurance and credit lines; check your state agriculture portal or bank for details.

Learn how to apply (short)
  1. Check scheme eligibility on the official portal.
  2. Prepare basic documents (land proof, ID, bank details).
  3. Apply online or through your local agriculture office / FPO.
  4. Follow up with the implementing agency for approval and disbursement.
FAQ
  • Q: Can small farmers apply? A: Yes — many schemes have provisions for smallholders or FPOs.
  • Q: Where to get help? A: Contact your district agriculture office or local FPO for guidance.

Farmer Storage Safety Checklist

  • Clean and dry store before placing produce
  • Keep produce off the floor and cover with breathable material
  • Ensure ventilation and avoid direct sunlight
  • Dry grains to safe moisture before long storage
  • Regularly inspect for pests and mold
