Importance

Safe storage keeps food and income secure. Reducing spoilage helps farmers earn more and supports local markets.

Problem

Many losses happen after harvest due to heat, moisture, and long storage. Small changes in storage can cut losses a lot.

Government Role

Government programs provide storage facilities, training, and subsidies. AgriSpectra works with these efforts — not against them — to make local storage decisions easier.

Our Solution

AgriSpectra gives a simple score and clear actions based on crop, temperature, humidity, and days in storage.

How It Works

Government Support & Useful Links

Official schemes and portals that support storage, cold chains, finance and farmer groups.

  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF) — Long-term financing for cold storage, warehouses, silos and post-harvest infrastructure. agriinfra.dac.gov.in — Registration
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) — Support to modernize food processing and build cold chains and storage. mofpi.gov.in / PMKSY — SAMPADA portal
  • Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) — Parent ministry for cold chain, food processing and storage programs. mofpi.gov.in
  • e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) — Online market platform where farmers can sell across markets; helps reduce storage time and spoilage. enam.gov.in
  • Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC) — Supports FPOs, farmer groups and agribusiness linkages. sfacindia.com
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) — Direct income support scheme for farmers; useful context for planning inputs and storage. pmkisan.gov.in

News & Awareness

Short news summaries can increase trust. Examples: new cold storage projects under PMKSY, government approvals for scheme expansion, and state-level storage targets. Use local news links for context.

Farmer Storage Safety Checklist

  • Clean and dry storage area before use
  • Keep grains off the floor on pallets
  • Monitor temperature and humidity daily if possible
  • Check for pests, mold or unusual smells weekly
  • Dry grains to safe moisture before long storage
