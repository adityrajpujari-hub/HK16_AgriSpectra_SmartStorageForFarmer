How It Works

Understand the science behind our risk assessment engine

AgriSpectra uses crop-specific scientific ranges, region adjustments, seasonality and storage duration to compute an explainable risk score. The engine is rule-based and auditable; it is ML-ready for future improvements.

Algorithm Summary

1

Crop Thresholds

Start with crop ideal temperature and humidity ranges.

2

Regional Bias

Apply adjustments based on climatic zone.

3

Compute Penalties

Calculate deviations in temperature, humidity and storage duration.

4

Adjust & Aggregate

Adjust risk by crop respiration and seasonality.

Scientific Foundation

AgriSpectra values use published agronomic thresholds based on decades of research:

  • Grains tolerate lower moisture
  • Fruits and sugarcane are highly perishable
  • Seasonal variations affect storage
  • Regional climate impacts storage conditions
