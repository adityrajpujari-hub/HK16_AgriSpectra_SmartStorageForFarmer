Farmer Storage Safety Checklist

  • Clean and dry storage area before use
  • Keep grains off the floor on pallets
  • Monitor temperature and humidity daily if possible
  • Check for pests, mold or unusual smells weekly
  • Dry grains to safe moisture before long storage
