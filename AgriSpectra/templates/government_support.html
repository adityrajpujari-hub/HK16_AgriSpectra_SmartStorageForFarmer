Government Support & Useful Resources for Farmers

The Government of India provides several schemes and infrastructure support to reduce post-harvest losses and strengthen agricultural storage. Farmers can explore the following official resources.

Storage & Infrastructure Support

  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF): agriinfra.dac.gov.in
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana: mofpi.gov.in / PM-Kisan SAMPADA
  • Ministry of Food Processing Industries: mofpi.gov.in

Digital & Market Support

  • e-NAM (National Agriculture Market): enam.gov.in
  • SFAC (for FPO support): sfacindia.com

Financial & Farmer Support

  • PM-KISAN: pmkisan.gov.in
  • Crop insurance portal: Can be added in a future update.
