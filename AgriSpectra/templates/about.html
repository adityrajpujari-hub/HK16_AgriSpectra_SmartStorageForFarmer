About AgriSpectra

Empowering farmers through science-based decision support

AgriSpectra is a simple, science-based tool that helps farmers and extension workers make safer storage choices. It uses clear rules and common measurements to give trustworthy advice. Built for a hackathon, it is ready for further testing and local validation.

Our Mission

Empower Farmers

Provide accessible, science-based tools to reduce post-harvest losses and improve storage decisions.

Science-Driven

Build on peer-reviewed research and established agronomic principles for transparent results.

Open & Collaborative

Support farmer networks and supply chain managers with explainable insights.

Technical Details

Built with:

  • Flask (Python backend)
  • HTML5 & CSS3 (Modern UI)
  • Chart.js (Data visualization)
  • Font Awesome (Icons)

Monetization & Sustainability

Suggested approach: core farmer-facing features are free (freemium). Revenue can come from paid dashboards for extension officers, partnerships with storage providers, and integration services for governments or agritech companies. Keep pricing simple and focused on value for smallholders.
